# GuardianAI: A Financial AI Safety Assistant

## Overview

GuardianAI is an AI-powered financial safety assistant designed to identify potentially harmful financial decisions before users act on them.

The system combines rule-based safety checks with Google's Gemini large language model to analyze financial questions, assess risk, explain potential harms, and recommend safer alternatives.

---

## Problem Statement

Many people receive financial advice from AI systems without fully understanding the associated risks.

GuardianAI aims to improve AI safety by detecting risky financial situations and encouraging safer financial decision-making.

---

## Features

- Detects high-risk financial questions
- Classifies financial risk categories
- Explains why a decision may be harmful
- Generates safer recommendations using Google Gemini AI
- Supports responsible AI-assisted financial decision-making

---

---

## Example

### User Question

> Should I borrow money to buy Bitcoin?

### GuardianAI Response

- **Risk Score:** 10/10
- **Risk Level:** Extreme
- **Category:** Debt Risk
- **Potential Harm:** Severe financial loss
- **Recommendation:** Avoid borrowing money to invest in highly volatile assets such as Bitcoin or other cryptocurrencies.
